Current, I'm pursuing Ph.D. degree in the Graduate Institute of Electrical Engineering of National Taiwan University and serves as a research assistant in the Research Center of Information Technology Innovation at Academia Sinica, Taiwan. My current research interests include embedded system, mobile computing and deep learning.
